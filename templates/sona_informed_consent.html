Consent Form

Please read this form carefully. The purpose of this form is to provide you with important information about taking part in a research study. If you have any questions about the research or any portion of this form, please ask me.

The person in charge of this study is Sejin Paik. Participation in this research is voluntary, which means that it is something for which you volunteer. It is your choice to participate in the study, or not to participate. If you choose to participate now, you may change your mind and stop participating later.

The purpose of this study is to understand how people feel when they come across certain news headlines and images. We are asking you to take part in this study because you are 18 years or older and are fluent in English. If you decide to take part in the study, you will be asked to answer questionnaires relating to news content. This survey should take around 1-2 hours.

The main risk of allowing us to use and store your information for research is a potential loss of confidentiality. We will protect your privacy by not asking any personal identification information, using only ID numbers to assign your results and by storing the collected information in a password-protected computer.

Only I and certain BU offices in charge of monitoring human subjects research for safety and internal auditing purposes will have access to identifiable information. These offices include BU Central Offices and the BU Institutional Review Board (BU IRB). If you have any questions or concerns about your rights as a research participant, please contact the BU IRB at irb@bu.edu.

If you have any questions about the study, please contact me or my faculty advisor at the contact information listed below:

  • PI: Sejin Paik, sejin@bu.edu
  • Faculty advisor: Dr. Lei Guo, guolei@bu.edu
  • Contact Hours: Mon-Fri., 8am-6pm

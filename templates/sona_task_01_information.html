Gun Violence Headline Emotion Detection

Hello, thanks for taking this task. The purpose of this task is to determine the emotion you feel towards gun violence related news. In particular towards gun violence related headlines. All of the headlines in this task originate from US news articles.

If you have any questions or comments regarding this task, feel free too reach out to me at: sejin@bu.edu.

Steps for the task:

Step 1:

You would be presented with a headline from US news article pertaining to gun violence, example: "Cremated remains of Las Vegas mass shooter to be kept in safe deposit box, brother says"

Step 2:

You would be asked about the emotion you feel on reading this headline. (Choose one of the button from the options given and the next question would appear.) Example:

Given the above news content (headline) in the context of gun violence, what is the dominant emotion that you feel?

Step 3:

You would be asked to choose the intensity of the emotion you selected in Step 2. You would be presented with a slider having options for intensity from 1 to 5, with 1 being most mild and 5 being most extreme. (Choose one amongst these three options and the next question would appear.) Example:

What is the intensity of your feeling?
